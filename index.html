Violin Go Learn Play About

Welcome to Violin Go

the game where you learn, play, and improve your violin skills by mastering note reading and finger positions!

How to Play

▼

The game displays a note, and you must select:

  • The correct note name (e.g., A, B, C, etc.)
  • The correct finger number:
    • 0 = Open String
    • 1 = Index Finger
    • 2 = Middle Finger (always specify High or Low)
    • 3 = Ring Finger
    • 4 = Pinky Finger
  • Select if the finger is Low (L) or High (H)
  • The correct accidental (sharp ♯ or flat ♭) when needed.

Important Rules

▼
  • Notes played with fingers 1, 3, or 4 on tapes only require selecting the finger number.
  • All 2's must be specified as High or Low.
  • If the note makes no sound, it means you're missing a selection—either note name, finger number, accidental, or High/Low option.

Scoring and Progress

▼
  • Start at Level 1. Earn 10 XP per level to unlock the next.
  • Each correct answer gives you 1 XP.
  • After unlocking a level, you can always revisit and practice previous levels.
  • Accuracy percentage is provided for your reference and does not impact scoring.
  • To restart your progress, simply reload the page and begin your violin journey again!

Game Levels

▼

Each game level corresponds to specific violin skills:

  • Level 1: Open Strings – Learn and practice the open strings of the violin.
  • Level 2: Notes on the A String – Learn notes A4, B4, C#4, D4 on the A string.
  • Level 3: Notes on the D String – Learn notes D4, E4, F#4, G4 on the D string.
  • Level 4: Notes on the E String – Learn notes E5, F#5, G#5, A5 on the E string.
  • Level 5: Notes on the G String – Learn notes G3, A3, B3, C3 on the G string.
  • Level 6: Notes on Tapes – Discover and play notes using fingers placed on tapes.
  • Level 7: Low 2's – Learn the low 2 finger position across all strings.
  • Level 8: High 3's – Master the high 3 finger position across all strings.
  • Level 9: Low 1's and Low 4's – Introduce low 1 and low 4 finger positions.
  • Level 10: G String Practice – Focus exclusively on all notes of the G string.
  • Level 11: D String Practice – Focus exclusively on all notes of the D string.
  • Level 12: A String Practice – Focus exclusively on all notes of the A string.
  • Level 13: E String Practice – Focus exclusively on all notes of the E string.
  • Level 14: Enharmonic Challenge – Master enharmonic equivalents across all strings.
  • Level 15: Ultimate Challenge – Test your knowledge of every note in the first position!

Enjoy learning and mastering the violin with Violin Go!
