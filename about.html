About ViolinGo

ViolinGo is an interactive web application designed to make learning the violin more engaging and fun. Through gamification and interactive exercises, users can practice note recognition, finger positions, and develop their musical ear.

Mission

Our mission is to make violin learning accessible, enjoyable, and effective for everyone, from complete beginners to intermediate players.
